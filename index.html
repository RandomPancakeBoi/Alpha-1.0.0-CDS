Who we are

CDS Group Ltd is a UK-based third-party logistics (3PL) provider serving retail, healthcare, and home delivery sectors. Combining operational scale with close client partnerships, we deliver consistently excellent experiences.

Built on Experience

Operating since 2015, CDS Group has steadily grown its fleet and capabilities while keeping a strong focus on service and safety.

Compliance & Capability

Registered in Nottinghamshire and operating under SIC 49410 (Freight transport by road), we meet industry standards for transportation and healthcare logistics.
